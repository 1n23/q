Dots Operator

 

The Dots Operator adds randomly distributed single pixel dots in two colors to a texture.

 

Inputs

 

One texture.

 

Parameters

 

Color 0 & Color 1

Define dot colors

 

Count

Controls the amount of dots

 

Seed

A unique identifier for the pseudorandom pattern
